RIP to my "bum" year.

After completing my master's degree in May of 2021 after four years of very hard work, I decided that I wasn't ready to get a "big boy" job. I was never passionate about math or statistics. Truth be told, I did math at UNLV because it was the hardest thing I could chose to do.

Some people are really fortunate to know what they are passionate about. Or at least what they want to do in life.

Some people are also really fortunate to find out what they are passionate about at some point along the way.

I was not so lucky.

I tried really hard to be passionate about math. Then I tried really hard to be passionate about insurance. And then I tried really hard to be passionate about statistics. At the end of the day, the only thing I was really doing with a passion was trying hard. So once I got all of my schooling out of the way, I decided that I was going to try really hard at being happy, collecting life experience, and hopefully finding some direction along the way.
